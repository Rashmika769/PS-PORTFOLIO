// FINAL REFLECTION

Closing Thoughts

The Professional Skills module reshaped my understanding of what it truly means to be a professional in the IT industry. I entered the module focused almost entirely on technical competence — but I am leaving with a much broader and richer perspective on professional excellence.

The lecture on Emotional Intelligence was transformative for me. Understanding that how we manage our emotions directly impacts our work quality, relationships and reputation made me reflect deeply on my own behaviour in group settings. I now approach challenges with greater emotional awareness and patience.

The Code of Ethics and Business Ethics sessions were equally eye-opening. As someone who intends to build software that millions of people may use, understanding the weight of ethical responsibility in computing was both humbling and motivating. I now see software development as an act of public trust, not just a technical exercise.

From Interview Skills to Negotiation, from Grooming to CV Writing — every lecture gave me practical tools I can apply immediately. This portfolio is the embodiment of all these lessons. It represents not just what I learned, but who I am becoming as a professional.

"Growth as a professional is not a destination — it is a deliberate, continuous practice of learning and reflection."
— Y.R.Jayasekara, 2026
